Choosing a subreddit

There are thousands of subreddits available to hold your ama, each with a different audience group. Search reddit.com and find a subreddit that best matches the topic of your AMA. If you want to submit a general AMA, choose reddit.com/r/iama.

Writing a great title

The title of your AMA should grab redditors attention and briefly describe who you are. You can mention a notable accomplishment, an upcoming event that you are involved in, or highlight your field of expertise. Make sure it relates to your subreddit audience.

Writing the body

The body text will only be seen by those who click on your title. This is your chance to elaborate on who you are, what you have accomplished, and why redditors should care to ask you questions. You can use the body of your AMA to guide questions towards a specific topic to keep the AMA focused. Your website or other works can be linked here to validate you and for subtle promotion.

Creating a verification link

Verifying your AMA adds credibility to your submission and will increase engagement with redditors. You can verify your AMA by making a post on your official website or social media accounts that includes your reddit username. Include a picture of yourslef holding up a "Hi reddit" sign for bonus points.
